# Promotion

## Shorts ideas

- Cold-open teaser (15-30s): open on “The day I learned the truth about my husband's affair with my best friend, everything changed.” then cut to black with “Full story on the channel.”
- The reveal (20-40s): build for 10 seconds, then drop the moment the betrayal is discovered. End on a question to drive comments.
- The payoff (20-40s): tease the setup in one line, then show the satisfying turn. Caption: “Would you have done the same?”
