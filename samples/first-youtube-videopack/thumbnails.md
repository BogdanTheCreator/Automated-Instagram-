# Thumbnail concepts (mobile-first, <=4 words of overlay)

1. Split frame: a warm family photo torn down the middle. Overlay 2 words like "SHE LIED" in bold condensed type, red accent. High contrast for mobile.
2. A hand hiding a phone behind a back, screen glowing. Overlay "THE TEXT". Dark, cinematic, single light source.
3. An empty chair at a dinner table with one place setting removed. Overlay "NOT INVITED". Moody, lots of negative space so it reads at thumbnail size.
